Web 4.0

Web 4.0, also known as the "Intelligent Web" or "Web of Things," represents the future evolution of the World Wide Web. It is characterized by the integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to create a more intelligent and interconnected web experience.

Web 4.0 aims to enable seamless communication and interaction between devices, applications, and users, creating a more personalized and context-aware web experience. It focuses on the use of AI-powered virtual assistants, smart devices, and interconnected systems to provide users with more relevant and personalized content and services.

With Web 4.0, users can expect a more immersive and interactive web experience, where applications can anticipate user needs and provide proactive assistance. It also has the potential to enable new forms of collaboration and data sharing, as well as enhance the capabilities of virtual assistants and other AI-powered applications.
